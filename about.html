About Us

Our mission is to make screen time safe & magical.

Who We Are

Kidofy was born from a simple idea: technology should be a bridge to creativity, not a barrier. We are a team of parents, educators, and developers passionate about building safe digital playgrounds.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, ad-free educational content that respects children's attention and privacy. We believe in learning through play, exploration, and curated video content that sparks curiosity.

The Team

Our team comprises experts in child psychology, early childhood education, and interactive design. Every feature in Kidofy is tested with real kids (and approved by real parents!).
