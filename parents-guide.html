Parents Guide

Take control and keep your children safe online.

Getting Started

Welcome to Kidofy! We're thrilled to help you create a safe digital environment for your children. Our app is designed with both kids and parents in mind, giving you complete control while letting your children explore and learn safely.

Parental Controls

Age Restrictions: Set content ratings based on your child's age to ensure age-appropriate videos are displayed.

Screen Time Management: Control daily viewing limits and set specific time windows when your child can use the app.

Content Filtering: Browse and approve specific content channels or categories before your child sees them.

PIN Protection: Secure parental settings with a PIN to prevent unauthorized changes.

Setting Up Your Account

Step 1: Download Kidofy from the App Store or Google Play.

Step 2: Create a parent account with your email address.

Step 3: Add child profiles and customize settings for each child.

Step 4: Share the child-friendly login with your child and enjoy!

Monitoring & Insights

View detailed reports about what your child is watching, how much time they spend on the app, and their favorite content categories. This helps you understand their interests and ensure healthy screen time habits.

Best Practices

Co-viewing: Watch content with your child when possible to discuss what they're learning.

Regular Reviews: Check your child's viewing history weekly to stay informed.

Open Communication: Talk to your child about what they watch and encourage questions.

Balanced Screen Time: Use our screen time limits feature to ensure healthy habits.
