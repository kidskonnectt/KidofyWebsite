Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Login

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

Q: Can I have multiple parent accounts?
A: Yes! You can add additional parent accounts with different email addresses to manage the same child profiles.

Content & Features

Q: How is content selected?
A: Our team of educators and child safety experts carefully curate content. All videos are reviewed for age-appropriateness and educational value.

Q: Does Kidofy have ads?
A: Kidofy is mostly ad-free. We keep ads minimal to maintain a distraction-free learning experience for children. Parental controls help manage what your child sees.

Q: Can I request specific content?
A: Absolutely! Visit our content request form in the app or email us at content@kidofy.in with your suggestions.

Technical Issues

Q: The app crashes on my device. What should I do?
A: First, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app. If the issue persists, make sure your device has the latest software update.

Q: Videos won't load. How do I fix this?
A: Check your internet connection. Try clearing the app cache or refreshing. If problems continue, contact our support team.

Q: Is Kidofy available offline?
A: Kidofy requires an internet connection to stream content. However, we're working on offline viewing features!

Subscriptions & Billing

Q: Is Kidofy free?
A: Kidofy offers a free version with access to curated content. Premium features are available with a subscription.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?
A: You can cancel anytime through your account settings. No hidden fees or long-term contracts!

Get in Touch

Email Support: contact@kidofy.in - We respond within 24 hours (sometimes it will take more than 2 days also)

Social Media: Follow us on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter for updates and quick responses
