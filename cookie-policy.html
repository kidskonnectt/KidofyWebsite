Cookie Policy

We prioritize your child's privacy and safety.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better experience and remember your preferences without compromising your privacy.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies: These are necessary for our website to function properly. They help us provide features like remembering your login information and website preferences.

Analytics Cookies: We use these to understand how visitors interact with our site so we can improve the experience.

Marketing Cookies: These help us show you relevant content and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Your Privacy Matters

We never collect personal information about children without parental consent. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR requirements. You can control cookie settings in your browser at any time.

Cookie Management

Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. You can also delete cookies from your device. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our cookie policy, please reach out to us at privacy@kidofy.in. We're here to help!
